Admissions Requirements

  • Minimum of five (5) O' Level credit passes including English Language and Mathematics.
  • UTME candidates must meet the required JAMB cut-off mark.
  • Direct entry applicants must possess a relevant A' Level, OND, HND, or equivalent qualification.
  • Successful completion of the TAU screening exercise.

How to Apply

  1. Visit the TAU Admissions Portal.
  2. Create an account and complete the online application form.
  3. Upload required documents (WAEC/NECO result, JAMB result slip, passport photograph, etc.).
  4. Pay the application fee online.
  5. Submit the application and await confirmation email.

Admissions FAQ

What programs does TAU offer?

TAU offers undergraduate and postgraduate programs across multiple faculties including Sciences, Arts, Management, Computing, and more.

Can I apply with awaiting results?

Yes, candidates with awaiting results can apply, but admission will be conditional until final results are submitted.

What are the tuition fees?

TAU offers affordable tuition. Detailed fee structures are available on the university website or at the admissions office.

Are scholarships available?

Yes, various merit-based and need-based scholarships are available. Details are provided during the admissions process.
